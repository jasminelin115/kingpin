{ OUR STORY }

Serving Berkeley since 1939.
King Pin Donuts is a family owned and operated business that has been around since the 1930's. We use fine ingredients and traditional methods to produce exceptional donuts, cakes, muffins, pastries and cookies. King Pin has been a pioneer in the baking industry, king pin donuts is the first to offer trans-fat free products in the United States and impact the quality of food offers to consumers. We continually strive for innovation at King Pin Donuts, while intending to bring quality, fresh service daily to the Berkeley community.
